About the Event

This unique overnight inter-university blogathon is designed to push your creative limits while testing your speed, adaptability, and teamwork in a high-pressure, high-energy environment.

Unlike traditional competitions, Type Till Sunrise unfolds over the course of a 12-hour overnight marathon. Competition Rounds

Round 1: Topic Takedown

Teams will select a predefined topic (first-come, first-served, max 5 teams per topic) and write a well-structured blog within 2 hours. The blog will be evaluated for relevance (auto-checked), originality (manual plagiarism check), and content quality. Submissions must be in Word format (.docx).

Topic Takedown Details

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Word Limit: 800-1000 words

Choose from limited topic slots and create a compelling blog post. Topics will be revealed at the start of the round.

Judging Criteria:
  • Content Quality (40%)
  • Creativity (30%)
  • Structure (20%)
  • Language (10%)

Round 2: Audible to Article

A typing speed challenge where one team member transcribes an audio clip in real time. The system will auto-calculate typing speed (WPM) and accuracy, with deductions for spelling errors. The winner will be announced immediately and awarded a medal and certificate on the spot.

Audible to Article Details

  • Duration: 1 hour
  • Min WPM: 40

Listen to audio content and transform it into a well-structured article while maintaining accuracy and adding creative elements.

Judging Criteria:
  • Accuracy (40%)
  • Formatting (30%)
  • Speed (20%)
  • Enhancement (10%)

Round 3: Eyes Shut, Words Flow

One team member dictates, and another blindfolded member types in a live text box. The system will automatically check spelling accuracy, typing speed, and topic relevance. The most accurate and fastest team will be declared winners immediately and awarded a medal and certificate.

Eyes Shut, Words Flow Details

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Team Coordination Required

One team member dictates while another types with eyes closed, testing trust and coordination.

Judging Criteria:
  • Typing Accuracy (35%)
  • Team Coordination (30%)
  • Content Quality (25%)
  • Time Management (10%)

Round 4: The Creator's Arena

Teams will choose their own topic (unique per team) and submit both a blog and a 2-minute pitch video. The system will auto-check relevance, while judges evaluate content quality and presentation.

Creator's Arena Details

  • Duration: 5 hours total
  • Word Limit: 1200 words

Create a comprehensive blog with multimedia elements and present through a pitch video.

Sub-rounds:
  • Ideation & Writing (3 hours)
  • Visual Enhancement (1 hour)
  • Video Pitch (1 hour)
Judging Criteria:
  • Content Quality (30%)
  • Visual Appeal (25%)
  • Presentation (25%)
  • Innovation (20%)
